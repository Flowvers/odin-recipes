Choco Chip Cookies

Description

Made with only 5 ingredients, these choclate chip cookies will be sure to not disappoint. Taking only 20 minutes, you can have soft, gooey, and delcious choclate chip cookies whenever you want.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • Â¾ cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Sift flour and brown sugar into a bowl; stir in eggs. Mix butter into flour mixture until dough is creamy; fold in chocolate chips.
  3. Spoon dough onto a baking sheet, keeping them about 3 inches apart. Bake in the preheated oven until slightly brown, 10 to 15 minutes. Cool.
